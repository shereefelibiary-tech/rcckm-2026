# RSS Methodology

RSS is RCCKM's cumulative risk-burden display. It is an interpretation layer,
not a replacement for PREVENT, CKM staging, CAC interpretation, or guideline
treatment logic.

## What RSS Shows

RSS contributors make visible which signals are adding burden, such as:

- Atherogenic lipids: LDL-C, ApoB, triglycerides, Lp(a).
- Plaque/CAC: CAC category or clinical ASCVD context.
- Kidney risk: eGFR and UACR.
- Metabolic risk: A1c, diabetes, BMI/metabolic context.
- Behavioral and contextual enhancers: smoking, family history, inflammatory
  disease, ancestry context, reproductive history, HIV/cancer context when
  present.

## What RSS Does Not Do

- It does not diagnose ASCVD.
- It does not convert missing plaque data into CAC 0.
- It does not replace domain-specific treatment rules.
- It does not independently trigger medication decisions.

RSS is intended to help clinicians see the risk pattern quickly and verify that
the plan matches the visible contributors.
