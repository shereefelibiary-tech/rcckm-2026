# PREVENT and CKM Notes

RCCKM uses PREVENT-era ASCVD risk estimates and CKM staging as complementary
context.

## PREVENT

PREVENT provides population-level risk estimates. RCCKM displays 10-year and
30-year ASCVD risk when inputs are sufficient.

RCCKM uses PREVENT risk as context for prevention intensity, not as the only
decision input. Plaque, severe LDL-C elevation, clinical ASCVD, diabetes,
kidney involvement, ApoB/Lp(a), family history, and other risk enhancers may
change the interpretation.

## CKM

CKM staging summarizes cardiometabolic and kidney involvement. RCCKM uses:

- A1c/diabetes and metabolic context.
- eGFR and UACR for kidney G/A staging.
- Clinical ASCVD when present.

## Important Boundaries

- Clinical ASCVD is a secondary-prevention context.
- CAC/plaque without clinical ASCVD remains subclinical plaque context.
- Low 10-year PREVENT risk should not de-risk severe LDL-C, clinical ASCVD, or
  high plaque burden.
- CKM/kidney recommendations should be tied to eGFR, UACR, diabetes, BP, and
  medication context.
